Our Story

How Crispy Kitchen became the beloved restaurant it is today

Crispy Kitchen began as a small family-owned diner in 2010, with just five tables and a simple menu. Our founder, Chef Michael Rodriguez, had a vision of creating a restaurant that served high-quality, delicious food in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Through word of mouth and our commitment to excellence, we gradually expanded. What started as a humble diner has now become one of the city's most popular dining destinations, with over 50 employees and seating for 120 guests.

Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our roots. We still prepare many of our dishes using Chef Michael's original recipes, and we maintain the same focus on quality ingredients and exceptional service that we had on day one.

Today, Crispy Kitchen continues to innovate while staying true to our core values. We're proud to be part of the community and look forward to serving you for many years to come.
